About Me

Born and raised in Illinois, my academic and employment journey has taken me to 5 states and the District of Columbia. From a Wildlife Researcher to Environmental Consultant into a career in Education as a Special Education teacher.

My developer journey started in earnest in 2016 when I graduated from Metropolitan Community College with an Associate's degree in Software Development. Immediately I was able to put what I learned to practice as a Computer Teacher in the Kansas City Missouri Public Schools system. In 2018, I started a position as an Application Engineer at Amusement Connect. The breadth of my experience has enabled me to take a leadership role in techincal support, software support and networking. I'm very excited to continue my journey with LaunchCode, both with an eye to use the skills I learn in my current position, or expand to a new role at a different company.
